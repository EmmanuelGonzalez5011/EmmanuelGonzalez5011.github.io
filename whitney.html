Welcome!

The whitney Houston Foundation is a non profit charity organization dedicated to helping
at risk youth who are interested in the arts. Our goal is to help as many children and
young adults to take part in after school music, dance, and theatre groups in hopes to
further encurage them to have an interest in the arts as well as boost their individual creativity.
The Whitney houston Foundation was founded shortly after the legendary whitney houston's tragic death
in 2012.
